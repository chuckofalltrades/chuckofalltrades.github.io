I need to break this down better...

    May 31, 2024 by Charles Palmer

    I'm trying to accomplish:

    • A place to host portfolio content in a variety of formats such as images, videos, or interactive web-based elearning content
    • An "About Me" resume type site to sell myself to potential clients and employers
    • A blog with content that documents my ideas, project processes, and thought leadership in support of the above

    With these limitations:

    • I do not want to spend money on this if possible
    • It must be easy to maintain
    • It must be flexible to support experimentation and demonstration of things I haven't thought of yet
    • It should be universally accessible and avoid hosts or domains that might be blocked by potential clients or employers

    Current state:

    • Hosting using Github Pages (free)
    • Managing blog and general site with Publii

    This is working ok but I have the following issues before calling it done:

    • My domain (charlespalmer.me) is apparently not a great idea for accessibility.  My current employer for instance flags it as suspicious, which prevent active content (like my elearning examples) from working.  I'm assuming this is the .me TLD.
    • I don't love the customization in Publii. I like the app and simplicity of authoring/editing, but unless I build a custom theme I'm pretty limited in style.

    Today I will work on the domain issue.
